Apple-nut strudel

Required ingredients (for 8 servings):

  • One package of yeast-free puff pastry
  • 4 green apples
  • 100 g walnuts
  • 100 g brown sugar
  • 4 tbsp powdered sugar
  • 20 g butter
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • Flour for dusting work surface

The following can be used when serving the dish:

  • Powdered sugar
  • Mint leaves
  • Vanilla ice cream

Cooking steps:

  1. Thaw the puff pastry. Preheat oven to 180 degrees Celsius. Line baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. Toast the walnuts in a dry skillet until they become fragnant, then let them cool slightly. Put the nuts into the blender cup, add brown sugar, and pulse until coarse crumbs form.
  3. Peel the apples, halve them, remove their seeds and cut each half into small cubes. Toss cubed apples with cinnamon and powdered sugar.
  4. Dust your working surface with flour. Divide the thawed pastry in two parts. Roll out each part into a thin rectangle measuring approximately 25 by 45 cm. Place one rolled-out piece on kitchen towel, brush top side lightly with melted butter, sprinkle evenly with nut mixture. Cover with another layer of pasrty and spread apple filling over it.
