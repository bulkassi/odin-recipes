The Semolina cake (aka Mannik)

Required ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • One glass of sugar
  • One glass of kefir or yogurt
  • One glass of semolina
  • 200 g butter
  • 1 tbsp baking powder

Cooking steps:

  1. Mix the eggs with sugar in the bowl.
  2. Mix kefir (or yogurt) with semolina in a separate bowl, let it brew for 20 minutes.
  3. Melt the butter.
  4. Mix all ingredients, then add the baking powder and mix once more.
  5. Transer the mixture into the form, bake it at 180 degrees Celsius in the oven for 40 minutes.
  6. Let the dish cool completely.
